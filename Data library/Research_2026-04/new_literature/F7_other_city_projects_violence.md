# F7 — Other City Projects / Violence — New Literature 2023-2026

1. **Bhatt et al. (2024) — READI Chicago QJE** — see F4.1 for full citation. Flagship 2024 RCT of CBT + transitional jobs in Chicago.

2. **Cure Violence systematic review team. (2025).** "A Systematic Review on the Effectiveness of the Cure Violence Approach." URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40922561/. DOI via PMC12420962. OA: Y. Design: systematic review (PubMed + CVG files through May 2025). Effect: mixed; some sites show 37-63% reductions in gun injury/victimization (e.g., S. Bronx, Baltimore Cherry Hill 56% reduction in killings), others null or increase. Relevance: honest evidence picture for IMAN's violence-reduction investments. Tier 1.

3. **Strange CC, Pugliese K, Ranjan S, Shah A. (2025).** "Sustaining Community Violence Intervention and Prevention (CVI): A State Readiness Assessment for Funding CVI Through Medicaid." *INQUIRY*. DOI: 10.1177/00469580251359502. URL: https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/00469580251359502. OA: Y. Design: policy case study. Relevance: IL Medicaid reimbursement pathway for IMAN's CVI work. Tier 3.

4. **Multi-state CVI Medicaid implementation team. (2025).** "Medicaid reimbursement for community violence intervention and prevention (CVI): a multi-state policy implementation case study." *Health & Justice* 13. DOI: 10.1186/s40352-025-00327-7. URL: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s40352-025-00327-7. OA: Y. Design: multi-state policy case study. Relevance: direct template for Illinois Medicaid CVI billing. Tier 2.

5. **CVI evaluation scoping review team. (2025).** "Evaluating Community Violence Intervention Programs: A Scoping Review Synthesizing Methods and Measures." *PMC* 12334825. URL: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12334825/. OA: Y. Design: scoping review, 1996-2023. Effect: 76.4% of outcome evaluations use only deficit-based measures; process measures increasingly used. Relevance: informs IMAN's own CVI evaluation design. Tier 2.

6. **Braga AA, Douglas S. (2024).** "San Francisco Violence Reduction Initiative Assessment: Interim Report." Board of State and Community Corrections. URL: https://www.bscc.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/SF-VRI-Evaluation-Dr.-Braga-2024.pdf. OA: Y. Design: quasi-experimental evaluation. Relevance: current Braga evaluation of GVI in a peer city. Tier 2.

7. **GIFFORDS Law Center. (2024).** "2024 Community Violence Intervention Legislation Year in Review." URL: https://giffords.org/analysis/2024-community-violence-intervention-legislation-year-in-review/. OA: Y. Effect: states appropriated $520M+ for CVI in 2024; federal 350 CVI grants ($800M) rescinded midstream. Relevance: funding landscape context. Tier 3.
