# F8 — Income Transfers / Funding — New Literature 2023-2026

1. **Vivalt E, Rhodes E, Bartik A, Broockman DE, Miller S. (2024).** "The Employment Effects of a Guaranteed Income: Experimental Evidence from Two U.S. States." NBER WP 32719. DOI: 10.3386/w32719. URL: https://www.nber.org/papers/w32719. OA: Y. Design: RCT (N=3,000; $1,000/mo × 3 yr). Effect: -$1,800/yr individual non-transfer income; -4.1 pp labor-market participation; -1-2 hr/week work; largest increase in leisure time. Relevance: largest US UBI RCT; sobering employment evidence that IMAN should engage directly with. Tier 1.

2. **Bartik A, Rhodes E, Broockman DE, Krause P, Miller S, Vivalt E. (2024).** "The Impact of Unconditional Cash Transfers on Consumption and Household Balance Sheets: Experimental Evidence from Two US States." NBER WP 32784. URL: https://www.nber.org/papers/w32784. OA: Y. Design: same RCT (consumption outcomes). Effect: +$300/mo household expenditures; MPC on non-durables 0.44-0.55; largest spending on housing, food, cars. Relevance: confirms cash enables food/housing stabilization. Tier 1.
