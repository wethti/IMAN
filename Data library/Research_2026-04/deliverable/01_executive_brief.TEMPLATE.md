## Honest limitations

- USALEEP LE data is 2010–2015; we cannot directly measure LE change on recent IMAN work.
- Most of the evidence we borrow from is from RCTs elsewhere; IMAN's *combined* effects will need in-house quasi-experimental evaluation.
- Hispanic-paradox and immigrant-paradox explanations for positive deviance have well-known data critiques (salmon-bias, return-migration). We present them as hypotheses, not settled findings.
