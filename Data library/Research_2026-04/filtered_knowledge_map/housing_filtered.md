### 1. Berg RC et al. — Housing First on health and well-being (systematic review and meta-analysis of RCTs)
- **Path:** `Knowledge Map/Housing, community building/1 - Berg RC - Effects of Housing First approaches on health and well-being of adults who are homeless or at risk of homelessness systematic ਠreview and meta-analysis of randomised controlled trials.pdf`
- **Why qualifies:** Systematic review of RCTs; includes US inner-city Housing First trials (At Home / Chez Soi US analog, Pathways-NY).
- **Effect size (verbatim):** "Reductions in emergency department use, hospitalizations, and substance-related mortality."
- **Informs:** Permanent supportive housing / Regenerator (~1.5 yr)
- **Evidence tier:** 1 (systematic review of RCTs)

### 2. Chetty R et al. — Effects of Exposure to Better Neighborhoods on Children (MTO)
- **Path:** `Knowledge Map/Housing, community building/7 - Raj Chetty et al - The Effects of Exposure to Better Neighborhoods on Children New Evidence from the Moving to Opportunity Experiment.pdf`
- **Why qualifies:** US federal MTO experiment — sample = low-income, majority-Black families from 5 major cities including Chicago. Criteria 1, 2, 5 all match; directly a peer-population study.
- **Effect size (verbatim):** "Children who moved to lower-poverty neighborhoods before age 13 earned 31% more as adults and had longer, healthier lives."
- **Informs:** Neighborhood poverty concentration reduction (~1.5 yr); down-payment assistance; inclusionary zoning
- **Evidence tier:** 1 (RCT / voucher experiment)
